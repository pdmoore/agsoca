AgileSoCal Meeting - Wednesday, May 19th, 6:00 PM Pacific

The Leader Within: move from Resistance to YES with Louria Lindauer


Who are we as Agile Leaders and how do we show up in the world, with our teams, and within organizations?

Have you wondered why some people or teams respond to more than others? Why sometimes the same techniques work from some people/teams and not others? Are you asking - "Why are they so resistant?"

This all has to do with the Agile Leader that lives within all of us. We will discover our Agile Leadership Stance and with which culture environments we are most comfortable and how to elevate our agile effectiveness. Come lets have fun and discover together more agile super powers!

Your Presenter:


For the past 25 years, Louria Lindauer has empowered and co-created “People First Environments”, where talents are celebrated and voices matter. As an international Executive Agile & Leadership Coach Louria utilizes her mastery of coaching, emotional intelligence, facilitation, teaching and mentoring to breathe life to transform ‘just surviving’ organizations into thriving. She is known to say, “It’s not if you will fall down, but when you do fall down; then How YOU Recovery?!” Therefore, we love who we are, what we do and do what we love!

Louria has lived every role in an agile organization from software engineer to organizational agility enterprise coaching and strategy. In her own company, SuccessAgility, she has worked with hundreds of organizations and Agilists. She has coached them to elevate not only their skill set but also to become the leaders that others want to be and foster collaboration and sustainable change and growth.

Louria fell in love with coaching when she took her first agile coaching course which lead her to co-active coaching and becoming a Co-Active Leadership Faculty. Coupled with her passion and ability to lead with courage, love, big laughter, play, faith, connection, creativity, and making the impossible possible. Overall, Louria is fiercely committed to unleashing your greatness, success and ah-ha moments for lasting transformation.
