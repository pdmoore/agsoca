AgileSoCal Meeting - Wednesday, February 19th 6:00 PM

Agile Mindset Workshop with Janet Lawrence

When: Wednesday, February 19th
Networking and food, 6:00 PM.
Activities start, 6:30 PM.

Join us at our next evening meeting where Janet Lawrence, Director of Corporate PMO for Experian in Costa Mesa, will be facilitating an agile mindset workshop. This hands-on workshop will focus on:

  • How to evolve your mindset to align with your skills
  • Embracing Agile values, principles, and behavior
  • Enable you to change how you work, how you think about working with others, and how you behave as part of an Agile team


Your Presenter:

Janet Lawrence is a successful agent for change and continuous improvement for the financial services sector for over 20 years, in addition to serving on several non-profit boards. Janet is currently a Director in Experian's Global Program Management and Operational Excellence group, in the Corporate PMO, where she supports a team of 60 portfolio, program and project managers, along with several global business lines, including the IT services organization.

Over the past few years Janet has been a driver for Agile within Experian, co-authoring and implementing the Global Project Management Governance Flexible Framework (methodology agnostic). Janet authored and delivered the Agile Mindset workshop across several global regions, including training others to deliver the workshop. Janet's educational background is in Business Management and she is a certified ScrumMaster and internal Agile coach.
