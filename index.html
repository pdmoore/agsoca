Design Sprint 101 and Lightning Decision workshop

Join us at our first meeting of 2019 where we'll learn about, and experience, Design Sprints. Design Sprints are a Google Ventures game plan for figuring out if a new feature or service will wow customers or not by utilizing design-thinking principles coupled with lean and agile methodologies to deliver a high-fidelity, user-tested prototype. In this session Jason LaManque will instruct us on the why's and how's of Design Sprints and then we'll engage in some hands-on practice.

Your Presenter:

Jason LaManque is a Product Manager at Duro Labs where he helps build a B2B SaaS product for hardware data management. Jason is a Certified Scrum Product Owner, Interaction Design Foundation Certified Usability Tester, and holds a Master's in International Business.
